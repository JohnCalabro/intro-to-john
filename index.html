About

When I knew nothing about coding, it was easy for me to assume a coding project was some sort of math problem. Like with coding, I follow a process that involves building something from the ground up, in the case of writing, I build a story. But before I can craft a complete narrative, I need to do research about what I am writing, the next step is world building. Then I develop a set of characters who possess meaningful motivations, and then set up challenges my characters face within the world I built from scratch. The prospect of being able to implement a completely new creative process, in order to build software and web content from the ground up excites me.
